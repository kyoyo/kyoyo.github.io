# 不定动词


## 不定动词当名词


`To take a trip around the world`

1. 名词不定式作主语

To marry her is my goal

plan purpse attempt goal aim ambition dream ideal wish hope

也可换成  It is my goal to marry her

  
2. 名词不定式作及物动词受词

I want to see him

He intends to try it

    
## 不定动词当形容词

I have something to do

## 不定动词当副词用
